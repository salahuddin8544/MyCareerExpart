Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How does My Career Expert refine job alerts to my profile?

Our service utilizes advanced AI to analyze job listings, identifying key details like language requirements, nationality restrictions, and experience levels.

This ensures you only receive job alerts that match your professional qualifications and preferences.

Q: What differentiates My Career Expert from other job platforms?

Unlike traditional job boards, My Career Expert applies AI technology to filter out irrelevant listings, providing you with only the opportunities that genuinely fit your career aspirations in the international development sector.

This in turn minimizes the time you spend sifting through irrelevant positions.

Besides, our platform is tailored specifically for professionals in international development, providing curated opportunities in global organizations like the UN, UNDP, and others.

Q: Can I change my job preferences and alert settings?

Yes, you can update your job preferences and alert settings at any time through your user profile.

This ensures that the job matches you receive remain relevant to your evolving career goals.

Update your profile and CV at any time to keep job matches relevant.

Q: What is included in the Professional Career plan?

The Professional Career plan provides full access to all functionalities of My Career Expert, including personalized job alerts, downloadable CV templates, and access to exclusive webinars and newsletters.

It’s available monthly or yearly, offering flexibility to fit your career planning.

Q: How does the referral program work?

Our referral program rewards you for spreading the word about My Career Expert.

Share your unique invitation code with friends and colleagues.

For each one who signs up for a paid subscription using your code, both you and they receive an additional month of Professional Career subscription, with no limit on referrals.

Q: Is there support for users who are new to international development careers?

Absolutely.

All subscription tiers offer access to resources like CV templates, periodic webinars, and newsletters filled with career advice and featured jobs to support professionals at all stages of their international development careers.
